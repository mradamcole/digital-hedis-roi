Assumptions used in this model
  • The model is annual recurring benefit in steady state, not implementation cost.
  • All dollar outputs are ROM estimates intended for planning, not actuarial certification.
  • Some buckets are hard-dollar savings, some are revenue/risk protection, and some are soft-dollar / redeployment unless you remove expense from the budget.
  • Overlap haircut is applied after the bucket roll-up to reduce double counting.
  • Low / high ranges are anchored to the ROM ranges from the earlier estimate and scale with your revised base assumptions.
Tip: start by editing the inputs below, then fine-tune the 8 bucket sliders or use the master dial.

Editable inputs
